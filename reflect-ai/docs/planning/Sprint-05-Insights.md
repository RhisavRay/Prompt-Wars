# Sprint 05 — Insights

**Sprint:** 05  
**Status:** Planned  
**Priority:** High  
**Estimated Duration:** 2–3 Weeks  
**Last Updated:** 30 June 2026

---

# Objective

Help users discover meaningful patterns across their journals without reducing their experiences to numbers or analytics.

Sprint 05 expands Reflect's perspective beyond individual entries. Rather than reflecting on a single journal, Reflect begins identifying recurring emotions, themes, and shifts that emerge over time.

The goal is not to tell users who they are.

The goal is to help them notice what they may not have noticed themselves.

---

# Product Goal

Transform individual reflections into long-term understanding.

Users should gradually develop a clearer picture of how their thoughts, emotions, and experiences evolve over weeks and months.

Insights should feel like discoveries rather than reports.

---

# Product Question

> Can Reflect help users recognize meaningful patterns while preserving their ownership of the story?

---

# Core Principle

Patterns are invitations.

Not conclusions.

Reflect should never tell users what something means.

It should simply reveal what appears to be happening.

Interpretation always belongs to the user.

---

# Features

## Feature 1 — Theme Detection

**Status:** Not Started

Identify recurring themes across journals.

Examples:

- Work
- Relationships
- Health
- Family
- Creativity
- Purpose
- Rest

Themes should emerge naturally from journal content rather than being manually selected.

---

## Feature 2 — Emotional Journey

**Status:** Not Started

Visualize emotional movement over time.

Examples:

- Frequently recurring emotions.
- Emotional variety.
- Gradual emotional shifts.
- Changing emotional patterns.

The goal is awareness rather than measurement.

Avoid assigning scores.

---

## Feature 3 — Weekly Reflection

**Status:** Not Started

Generate a weekly reflection summarizing the past week's journals.

Possible content includes:

- Frequently discussed themes.
- Emotional movement.
- Gentle observations.
- Encouragement for continued reflection.

Weekly reflections should feel personal rather than analytical.

---

## Feature 4 — Monthly Reflection

**Status:** Not Started

Generate a broader monthly summary.

Examples:

- Major themes.
- Significant emotional changes.
- Moments of growth.
- Questions worth revisiting.

These summaries should become meaningful milestones over time.

---

## Feature 5 — Personal Patterns

**Status:** Not Started

Highlight recurring observations.

Examples:

"You often begin writing feeling uncertain before describing moments of clarity."

"Gratitude appears frequently when writing about family."

Patterns should always be phrased as observations rather than facts.

---

## Feature 6 — Gentle Insights

**Status:** Not Started

Surface occasional insights throughout the application.

Examples:

"You've mentioned creativity several times this month."

"This week contained more hopeful moments than last week."

Insights should remain infrequent so they continue feeling meaningful.

---

# Success Criteria

Sprint 05 is complete when:

- Cross-journal analysis functions correctly.
- Weekly reflections are generated.
- Monthly reflections are generated.
- Themes are identified consistently.
- Personal patterns feel helpful rather than intrusive.
- Insights preserve uncertainty and humility.

---

# Out of Scope

This sprint intentionally excludes:

- Long-term AI memory
- Anniversary journals
- Reflection timeline
- Personal milestones
- Export experiences
- Production optimization

Those belong to later sprints.

---

# Risks

Potential challenges include:

- False pattern recognition.
- AI overconfidence.
- Users treating observations as diagnoses.
- Repetitive summaries.
- Information overload.

Insights should always remain lightweight, optional, and clearly presented as observations.

---

# Dependencies

Requires:

- Completed Sprint 04
- Stable AI Reflection pipeline
- Reflection history
- Theme extraction
- Reliable emotional metadata

---

# Definition of Done

Every feature must:

- Follow the Reflect Handbook.
- Follow the AI Philosophy.
- Preserve user autonomy.
- Avoid deterministic language.
- Pass TypeScript.
- Pass ESLint.
- Pass `npm run build`.
- Be manually tested.
- Remain accessible.
- Present insights as possibilities rather than truths.
