# Purpose

This document captures ideas for Reflect before they are evaluated.

Ideas should be recorded as soon as they arise.

An idea does **not** need to be complete.

An idea does **not** need to be technically feasible.

An idea does **not** guarantee future implementation.

The purpose is simply to preserve inspiration.

---

# How to Use

When a new idea appears:

1. Write it down.
2. Keep the description short.
3. Do not discuss implementation.
4. Do not prioritize it here.

Ideas will later be reviewed and may move into the Product Roadmap or Backlog.
